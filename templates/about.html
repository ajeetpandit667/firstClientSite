Meet Isabella Rodriguez

With over 12 years of experience in the beauty industry, Isabella has transformed the faces of countless brides, models, and celebrities. Her passion for enhancing natural beauty while creating stunning, camera-ready looks has made her one of the most sought-after makeup artists in the city.

Isabella believes that makeup is not just about covering imperfections, but about celebrating each person’s unique features with artistry, care, and confidence. Every product is applied with precision, creating looks that are both timeless and trendsetting.
